Recommended Retailers

This is a list of my recommended retailers. These websites are all safe, reliable, and reputable. Shipping times are very reasonable and products arrive securely.

Amazon

Everyone knows Amazon. They usually have most parts available at the same price as other websites. Sometimes some parts are sold at higher price. If this is the case, I usually won't use Amazon.

Go to Site
Newegg

Newegg is a longstanding company known for selling PC parts. They carry basically every part. They usually sell all items at the market price.

Go to Site
Microcenter

Microcenter is probably my favorite of all the retailers listed. Unfortunately, Microcenter is not available everywhere, so if you don't live by one you have to have your items shipped. Not all of their items can be shipped. There are only 25 Microcenter locations around the U.S. If there is one near you, I highly recommend checking them out.

Go to Site
Best Buy

Best Buy doesn't carry all the parts that you may need. However, they do have the vast majority of them. Best Buy is a great site to use when looking for PC parts, however it usually is not my first choice.


Go to Site
Adorama

Adorama is very similar to Newegg. They carry supplies for most of your tech needs. Their PC part section is expansive and will have most, if not everything, you need.

Go to Site
B&H

B&H is another great store, especially if you live in the NY area. Their superstore is located a couple blocks from the Empire State Building.
